Featured Projects

Detailed case studies demonstrating practical structural solutions

01

Crane Gantry Foundations

Glass Manufacturer BS EN 1992 Constructed

The Challenge

Existing foundation and plinth ran close to proposed crane column positions, creating eccentric loading conditions that varied along the building length.

My Contribution

  • Developed three foundation types to suit varying proximity to existing foundation
  • Designed RC pad foundations — bearing, sliding, overturning checks
  • Designed RC strap beams for combined bending, shear and torsion
  • Detailed reinforcement with closed torsional links at 150mm centres
  • Prepared AutoCAD drawings as part of coordinated design package

Lesson Learned

Balancing structural efficiency against buildability — a single "optimal" solution wouldn't suit all site constraints.

02

Parapet Wall Stabilisation

Commercial BS EN 1993 Installed

The Challenge

No drilling or mechanical fixings permitted through the roof. Existing waterproofing meant any penetration would cause leakage.

My Contribution

  • Developed free-standing restraint using counterweighted steel buttress frames
  • Designed spreader beams to distribute loads across timber joists
  • Specified resin-fixed anchors into inner parapet face only
  • Prepared fabrication-ready steelwork drawings with bolted details
  • Coordinated safe installation sequence with contractor

Design Innovation

Counterweight system eliminated all roof penetrations — understanding the client's real constraint, not just the structural problem.

03

Shopfront Portal Frame Conversion

Retail BS EN 1993 Constructed

The Challenge

Existing blockwork infill panels being replaced with full-height glazing. Diagonal cross-bracing providing lateral stability conflicted with the new layout.

My Contribution

  • Designed moment frame using new UB beam between existing columns
  • Detailed bolted front plate and doubler plate to UC column
  • Designed Hollo-Bolt connection to SHS — no internal access needed
  • Integrated RSA brick support angle with glazing fixings
  • Prepared connection calculations and fabrication drawings

Lesson Learned

Connection design isn't just about strength — access, sequence, and tolerances matter just as much on a live retail site.

04

Residential Underpinning

Social Housing BS EN 1992/1997 £25K Saving

The Challenge

Tree removal had altered the moisture regime in shrinkable clay, creating both immediate subsidence and future heave risk.

My Contribution

  • Reviewed site investigation data and level surveys
  • Designed mass concrete underpin blocks below seasonal moisture zone
  • Specified Cordek heave protection void formers
  • Prepared technical recommendations with construction sequencing
  • Detailed isolation joints for future ground movement accommodation

Client Saving

£25,000 saved — Targeted underpinning retained existing structure, avoiding full rebuild. Heave protection future-proofed the solution.
