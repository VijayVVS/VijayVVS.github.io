Structural Fragility Analysis
— ✦ —
Research Detail
  • Enhanced reliability of fragility assessments by integrating forward uncertainty quantification with traditional fragility analysis
  • Developed a point source stochastic seismological model using MATLAB with Monte Carlo simulations to study uncertainties from seismological models to structural response
  • Conducted inelastic modal decomposition for random vibration analysis on non-proportionally damped, nonlinear MDOF structures using ANSYS
  • Findings contribute to more reliable design and assessment practices for civil infrastructure under seismic loading
Significance

More reliable fragility curves directly improve seismic risk assessments for bridges, dams, and critical infrastructure.
